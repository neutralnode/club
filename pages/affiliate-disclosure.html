Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: May 2026

AI Girlfriend Club earns affiliate commissions from some of the apps and services reviewed on this site. These commissions never influence which apps we recommend, how we rate them, or what we choose to test.

How affiliate relationships work on this site

When you click certain links on this site and subsequently make a purchase or sign up for a service, we may receive a commission from the company at no additional cost to you. This is a standard practice in review publishing and helps fund the independent testing and editorial work that goes into every review.

What affiliate relationships do not affect

Our editorial conclusions are based on direct testing of each app. Affiliate partnerships do not determine which apps we review, what scores or verdicts we assign, what problems we highlight, or whether we recommend an app at all. We have written negative conclusions about apps with which we have affiliate agreements, and we have recommended apps from which we earn nothing.

How to identify affiliate links

Affiliate links on this site are standard links to the apps or services reviewed. We do not use deceptive tracking or disguise affiliate links as editorial mentions. If you prefer to visit an app directly without using our link, you can do so — our review will not change.

FTC disclosure compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials. We are committed to transparency about how this site generates revenue.
